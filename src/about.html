What We're About
We want to form a group of friends who enjoy hanging out and love shredding down the mountains!

Club Meeting Breakdown
As a whole we will decide the destination and frequency of our trips. The meetings will be spent planning out details of the upcoming trips and just getting to know eachother better.

Who Can Join?
Anyone is welcome with open arms. A love for the slopes is always encouraged.
